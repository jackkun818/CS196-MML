The Tiananmen Gate Tower is one of the most magnificent city towers in ancient China. It is located at the northern end of Tiananmen Square, the capital of Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China, and the dividing point of the four regions of Dongcheng, Xicheng, Chongwen and Xuanwu. It is facing Chang'an Street. Opposite is Tiananmen Square, the Monument to the People's Heroes, Chairman Mao Memorial Hall, the Great Hall of the People, and the National Museum of China. It has attracted the attention of the world for its outstanding architectural art and special political status. On October 1, 1949, Chairman Mao Zedong solemnly declared:“The People's Republic of China was founded”, and personally raised the first five-star red flag. The Tiananmen Gate Tower pattern appears in the national emblem of the People's Republic of China and becomes a symbol of the People's Republic of China.
